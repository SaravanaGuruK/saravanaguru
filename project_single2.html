Job Search App and Responsive Website Design
Case Study

Dream Job is an online job search App and Responsive Website, It helps you in finding the right job and makes you shine in your career with the latest industry & job insights.

  • Project Duration : November 2021 - December 2021
  • The Problem : Job seekers and young college grads are finding it hard to find a job and are unable to obtain a job that suits their knowledge.
  • The Goal : Design an App and Responsive Website for a job search that facilitates access to job opportunities and a greater chance of getting hired.
  • My Role : Solo UX/UI Designer
  • Responsibilities : Conducting interviews, paper and digital wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, and iterating on designs

User Research: Summary

I conducted user interviews and unmoderated research on five people. Most of the research was conducted online. Most interview participants expressed frustration at not being able to find companies and proper jobs. Research has shown that users are open and willing to work toward reducing job search time if they had access to an easy-to-use tool to help guide them.

Persona 1: Alex
Problem Statement

Alex is a new college grad who needs best designing job for his startup career because he can’t get a job for his knowledge.

Persona 2: Katherine
Problem Statement

Katherine has the best software skills who needs the best company for work and more money because she can’t get a proper job for her work and salary.

Competitive Audit

An audit of a few competitor’s products provided direction on gaps and opportunities to address with the Job Search App and Responsive Website.

Ideation

I did a quick ideation exercise to come up with ideas for how to address gaps identified in the competitive audit. My focus was specifically on Searching for a job post and Apply a job.

Usability Study: Findings

These were the main findings uncovered by the usability study :


1. Search - Users want to search a job quickly.
2. Job post - User want to find their interested job in list of job post.
3. Apply - Before the apply process user need a quickly review and update their profile for apply a job.
