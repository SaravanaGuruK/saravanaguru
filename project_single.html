Corporate Project

CIM 1.0
Case Study

CIM is an application that helps Beginner investors, People who have zero knowledge in trading & People who failed to make a profit in trading to make more profit by using of CIM trading strategy.

  • Project Duration : August 2021 - Current
  • The Problem : People who have no knowledge of trading or a non-working strategy have been unable to earn money and make a profit in trading.
  • The Goal : Design a desktop and mobile application to enable people who have no experience in trading and who have failed to make a profit to become more successful investors.
  • My Role : Solo UX/UI Designer
  • Responsibilities : Conducting interviews, paper and digital wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, and iterating on designs

User Research: Summary

I conducted user interviews and unmoderated research on four people. Most of the research was conducted online. Most interview participants expressed frustration at not being able to make money and make a profit in trading. Research has shown that users are open and willing to invest their money toward wasting their time for the study about trading by using trading courses if they had access to an easy-to-use tool to help them to earn more money and profit.

Persona 1: John
Problem Statement

John is a junior developer who needs an online trading platform to earn more because he cannot save money.

Persona 2: Alex
Problem Statement

Alex is a business analyst who needs to make money in trade and make more profits because his trading plan doesn't work.

Persona 3: David
Problem Statement

David is a retired bank officer who needs guidance to make money through trading because he doesn't know anything about trading.

Persona 4: Steven
Problem Statement

Steven is an Administrator who needs a proper system for monitoring users because he is responsible for the user who invests in the trade.

Digital Wireframes

After ideating and drafting some paper wireframes, I created the initial designs for the Chiru Investment Web Application. These designs focus on the User's investing their money, Total Investment, Plan, and Investment History.

Usability Study: Findings

These were the main findings uncovered by the usability study :


1. Invest - Users want to be able to invest and withdraw on the same page.
2. Wallet - Users want to top up their wallets and withdraw money from their wallets on the same page.
3. Report - When users encounter problems with an application, they want to report a problem.
